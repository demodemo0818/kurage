## Full description (max 4000 characters)

Drift freely through the sea of the Fediverse.

Kurage is an unofficial client app for a more comfortable Mastodon
experience. Unified multi-account timelines, freely arranged multi-column
layouts and extensive customization take your Fediverse experience to the
next level.

■ Manage multiple accounts together
Register several Mastodon accounts and merge their timelines into a single
"unified timeline". Boost, favourite and reply seamlessly across accounts.
Each account can be assigned its own colour, so you can tell at a glance
which account a post came from.

■ Freely arranged multi-column UI
Arrange whichever timelines you like — home, local, federated, lists,
hashtags — side by side in a deck layout. Columns sit side by side on
desktop and switch via tabs on mobile, adapting to the screen size.

■ Rich posting features
- Cross-post to multiple accounts
- Live-post mode
- Scheduled posts
- Save drafts
- Quote posts (supports the official format from Mastodon 4.4 onward)
From everyday posts to live event coverage, Kurage covers it all.

■ Finely tunable display
Customize the post tile font, line spacing, custom emoji scale and more.
Switch between dark / light / system theme and change the theme colour to
match your taste.

■ Notifications and privacy
- Push notifications supported (Android)
- Optional app lock with PIN / biometric authentication

※ This app is an unofficial third-party client, not provided by Mastodon
gGmbH. A Mastodon account is required to use it.
